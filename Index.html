Saumya Kinnari Kartik Dalal


About me



Saumya Kinnari Kartik Dalal is a 12-year-old boy, born in the year 2009, studying in grade 7th at Kapol Vidyanidhi International School ICSE. He lives in a Hindu Gujarati joint family. His loved name is Somu. His keen interest is in dance and music. He likes to write poems. He has worked as a co-author in more than 65 anthologies. He writes poems in Hindi, Gujarati & English Languages. He had compiled two anthologies and is also an author of मेरी परछाई मेरी कविताएँ, his first solo book. He sees himself in the future as a good poet.
